Problems due to traffic conjesion

Traffic jam is the situation when vehicles are stopped completely for some time period on the roads.
Also, vehicles have to wait for a long time to move out of the jam. Sometimes it becomes like congestion in traffic.
This happens in transport network due to the increasing vehicles and overuse of roads. Often it is due to slow speed, longer trip time and increased queues of vehicles.
Therefore, traffic jam is becoming a major issue mostly in all cities.
